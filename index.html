About Me

Joy Maderazo, HD Quebec

Dentistry has always been a part of my life. I graduated dental school in the Philippines but then I immediately moved to Canada in 1990. For a while, I thought I was done with dentistry until I found a job in a dental clinic as an assistant. It was then that I realized that dentistry and helping people are part of who I am and I would love nothing more than to devote my professional career to it.

01

A New Path in Dental Hygiene

I decided to take dental hygiene and graduated from John Abbott College of Dental Hygiene in Montreal, Quebec in 2010. I began working as a dental hygienist for the next 10 years. In this environment, I honed my skills and learned many ways to treat clients.

02

Seeking Autonomy and Personalized Care

After 10 years working for a private dental clinic, I found myself looking for more autonomy to treat and see my clients according to my experience and my convictions. I believe in individualized care and work diligently to be up to date with current dental trends and technology.
